Featured Charities:


  • The Water Project

    The Water Project is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing access to clean, safe, and reliable water to communities in sub-Saharan Africa. Their efforts focus on sustainable water projects that improve health and quality of life for thousands of people.


  • The Humane Society

    The Humane Society works tirelessly to protect animals from cruelty, neglect, and exploitation. They advocate for stronger animal protection laws, promote animal adoption, and support rescue efforts across the country. Their mission is to create a more compassionate world for all animals.


  • Sophie's Squad

    Sophie's Squad is dedicated to raising awareness about suicide prevention and mental health issues, especially in and around female athletes. They provide resources, support, and education to help individuals in crisis and reduce the stigma surrounding mental health in athletes.
